#### EasyEats

EasyEats is a full-stack web application that helps college students quickly discover simple, affordable recipes and generate grocery lists with real-time pricing. By integrating recipe recommendations with grocery store data, the platform streamlines the entire process from meal planning to shopping.
[[Repository]](https://github.com/abdulkos786/EasyEats)

#### CivilSync

CivilSync is a web platform that helps users understand how their personal values align with real government actions. By analyzing executive orders and congressional data, the platform allows users to compare their views on key issues with actual policies and decisions, making civic engagement more accessible and transparent.
[[Repository]](https://github.com/abdulkos786/CivilSync)

#### BMW Repair

For many years, our family had a 2006 BMW 325i sitting in our garage. It was practically untouched for almost a decade, which also meant it had rarely ever been maintained. Over the course of a few months, I worked with my dad to do some serious repairs to the old beast, including suspension and shock replacement, as well as some minor oil leak fixes.

#### Museum Guide Robot

With a group of three other engineers at Michigan, I built a prototype Museum Guide Robot with successful line detection capabilities. This robot had a goal of leading visitors around museums in a guided tour manner, with visitors being able to select which exhibits they would like to see. We also planned for the robot to have an interactive touch screen where they could learn more about the exhibits while they visit them. While we didn't meet every objective, we still made considerable progress and I was able to learn a lot of valuable skills like PCB customization, soldering as well as basic Arduino C programming.
